About Me

My name is Julien. I am a 29-year-old Frenchman currently living in Bangkok, Thailand. I've been self-employed for the best part of my work life. At my first «big» job, my boss, Marc, who was the company's CEO, taught me a great deal. I've been set off to a good start for my projects.

As a former business owner, I look at the big picture and not only focus on the macro level of a project. I am used to working under pressure, and I am definitely a go-getter.

As a developer, I have the propensity to anticipate problems early. This allows me to plan lots of possible outcomes to a situation. I am rational in the decision-making process and I am thorough in my work.
